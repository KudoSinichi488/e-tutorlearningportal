About E-Tutor Learning Portal

The Electronic Tutor Learning Portal is a localized digital solution designed to bridge the gap between classroom instruction and independent study. By centralizing modules, grade tracking, and class management, we empower both teachers and students with a streamlined, organized, and accessible learning environment.

This platform is built for growth. At the start of every school year, accounts are automatically synced with specific subjects and sections, ensuring a "ready-to-learn" experience from day one.
